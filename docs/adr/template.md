## Context

What force compels this decision? Surface the constraints, the problem, and the state of the world today. Do not present the chosen solution here.

## Decision

State the decision in one or two sentences, verb-led: "We will use X." Follow with enough detail to make the decision implementable without additional guesswork.

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — one-sentence description. **Rejected because:** one-or-two-sentence reason.
- **Option B** — one-sentence description. **Rejected because:** one-or-two-sentence reason.
- **Option C** — …

Include at least one rejected alternative. An ADR with no alternatives is a post-hoc justification, not a decision record.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what this makes easier, cheaper, or safer.
- **Negative:** what this makes harder, more expensive, or riskier. Name real costs, not hedges.
- **Neutral:** what changes without being clearly better or worse.

## Revisit trigger

Under what observable condition should we re-open this decision? Examples: "if Apple ships X," "if >Y% of users are on Z," "if shipping this decision's follow-on work takes longer than W weeks."

If there is no conceivable trigger, say so explicitly and explain why.
