What Lumen is

Lumen is a native SwiftUI product for iPhone and iPad focused on local and privacy-first AI workflows. It supports on-device Apple Intelligence paths on supported hardware and user-configured Ollama endpoints in a local environment.

Privacy-first

No account is required to launch the app, and the shipped build does not include developer-operated analytics or ad-tech SDKs.

Native workflow

The product is built as a real Apple-platform app with local storage, accessibility work, and release documentation kept in the repository.
