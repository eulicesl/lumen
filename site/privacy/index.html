Lumen Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Lumen is designed as a privacy-first native app. The shipped product does not require an account, does not include analytics, and does not route conversation data through a developer-operated cloud backend.

Data stored locally

  • Conversations and message history
  • Optional memory entries saved for cross-conversation context
  • App preferences and local model configuration
  • Imported document text used to support a conversation

This information is stored using Apple platform storage APIs such as SwiftData and UserDefaults.

AI processing

  • Apple Intelligence or Foundation Models on supported devices
  • User-configured Ollama endpoints in a local environment

When you use Ollama, requests are sent only to the endpoint you configured. In the intended setup, that endpoint is local to your device or to your network, not a developer-operated cloud service.

Permissions

  • Microphone, for voice input you explicitly start
  • Speech Recognition, to transcribe voice input
  • Camera or Photo Library, when you choose to attach an image
  • Files, when you choose to import a document

Permissions are optional and used only for the feature you invoke.

Data sharing

Lumen does not sell personal data and does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics in the shipped app.

If you configure Lumen to talk to a service outside your device or local network, you are responsible for that service's privacy and security posture.
